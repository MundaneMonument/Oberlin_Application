Dear Pipo

I am writing to express my great interest in the Visiting Assistant Professor for Integrated Media that was posted on your site. As you can see from my CV, I have experience teaching both in traditional and new media. My own experience in art has taught me to combine video, net-art, archival arts, creative coding and 3D printing.

During the last semester I have taught four sections of a class on digital imaging and web-art. It was a mandatory class in the first year program aimed at the introduction of both new types of media and the idea of internet presence and citizenship. I developed all the classes which I taught. Most students have never thought of their computer as a productive tool, and this realization and the new uses they found were fascinating and enlightening. In the previous year, I have assisted students in various classes, both frontal art history seminars and project based classes. I was a teacher's assistant to Prehistory of New Media that explored the history of New Media and code art prior to the title origin in the 1980's. I also assisted graduate level seminar on experimental approaches to writing and taking writing off the page in which I was exposed to various methods of collaborations. I also learned teaching techniques to incorporate difficult texts into a project oriented seminar.
